Our Products

Innovative Alu Foils Packaging. We made just the most advanced and value added Alu Foils Packaging.

PHARMACEUTICALS

  • To cater to the demand of packaging requirement of pharmaceutical industry, We manufacture an extensive range of printed laminated pet, foil & poly film for making pouches of various sizes.

FMCG

  • Our range of printed and laminated flexible packaging material used for confectionery, Snacks, Coffee, Spices, pickles and others. We also manufacture and supply cut sheets of paper and foil flexible packaging in various shapes and sizes.

FOOD

  • We offer aluminium cut sheets and pouches for hotel, restaurants and airlines industry. We offer a gamut of aluminum packaging material for all kinds of foods. These aluminum foil containers are made from food grade aluminum, ensuring safety of the food and sustainability of the original taste.

CABLE INDUSTRY

    We are one of the leading manufacturer of aluminium mylar tape for cable industry. We can supply printed rolls in 6mm width and desired diameter.

CUT SHEET

  • We manufacture and supply a wide variety of aluminium foil cut sheets for use of Chocolate wrapper, decorative packaging and packaging for fire-works material. These sheets are fabricated using world-class raw materials and available in various specifications at affordable prices.

ROLLS

  • We manufacture a wide range of printed flexible material in rolls of various sizes and diameter. These are used for online and offline packaging of wide variety of materials including pharmaceuticals, foods, confectionery etc.

POUCHES

    We are instrumental in manufacturing and supplying a wide range of pouches made from aluminium, laminated with foil and paper. These pouches are fabricated using top quality materials and advanced technology, which increase their strength and provide longer shelf life. We have developed these pouches in such a manner that they confirm all quality standards in sufficient manner.

OTHERS

  • We have expertise in manufacturing of Cut Sheets for mehandi cone. we also make decorative printed aluminium sheets for fire works. Our range of Mehandi Cone Making Paper is available in different prints and shades as per the requirements.
